# Settings Guide

Customize your Koda experience in the settings area.

## Available Settings

### Profile Settings
- **Name**: Update your display name
- **Email**: View or change your email address
- **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture

### Language Preferences
- **Interface Language**: Choose your preferred language
- **Response Language**: Set the language for AI responses

### Notification Settings
- **Email Notifications**: Toggle email alerts
- **Processing Alerts**: Get notified when documents finish processing

### Security Settings
- **Password**: Change your account password
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable extra security
- **Session Management**: View and manage active sessions

### Account Management
- **Storage Usage**: View your storage quota
- **Export Data**: Download all your data
- **Delete Account**: Permanently remove your account

## How to Access Settings

1. Click on your profile icon in the top right
2. Select **{{nav.settings}}** from the dropdown
3. Or use the sidebar navigation

## Tips

- Enable two-factor authentication for better security
- Regularly review your active sessions
- Check storage usage to manage your quota
