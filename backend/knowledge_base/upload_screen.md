# Upload Guide

Upload your documents to start asking questions about them.

## Supported Formats

- **PDF**: Reports, contracts, articles
- **Word (.docx)**: Documents, letters, memos
- **Excel (.xlsx)**: Spreadsheets, financial data
- **PowerPoint (.pptx)**: Presentations, slides
- **Images**: PNG, JPG, JPEG (with OCR support)
- **Text**: Plain text files

## How to Upload

### Method 1: Drag and Drop
1. Open the upload area
2. Drag files from your computer
3. Drop them into the upload zone

### Method 2: Click to Browse
1. Click the **{{btn.upload}}** button
2. Select files from your file browser
3. Click open to start upload

## Upload Settings

- **Destination Folder**: Choose where to save your files
- **Auto-process**: Documents are automatically indexed for search

## Processing Time

- **Small files** (< 5MB): Usually instant
- **Large files** (> 50MB): May take a few minutes
- **Complex PDFs**: Processing includes OCR and formatting

## Tips

- Upload related documents together for better context
- Use clear file names for easy identification
- Check the processing status in the document list
