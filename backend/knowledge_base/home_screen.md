# Home Screen Guide

The home screen is your central hub for quick access to all features.

## Key Features

- **Quick Search**: Use the search bar at the top to find documents instantly
- **Recent Documents**: See your most recently accessed files
- **Storage Overview**: Monitor your storage usage at a glance
- **Quick Actions**: Access upload and folder creation with one click

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to navigate to other sections:
- **{{nav.documents}}**: Manage all your files and folders
- **{{nav.upload}}**: Add new documents
- **{{nav.chat}}**: Ask questions about your documents
- **{{nav.settings}}**: Configure your preferences

## Tips

- Pin frequently used documents for quick access
- Use keyboard shortcuts for faster navigation
- Check the activity feed for recent changes
