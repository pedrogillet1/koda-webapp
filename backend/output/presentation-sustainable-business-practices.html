Understanding Sustainable Business Practices

  • •Definition: Operating in a manner that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.
  • •Key Dimensions: Encompasses environmental protection, social equity, and economic viability.
  • •Beyond Compliance: Moving from regulatory adherence to proactive value creation and innovation.
  • •Strategic Imperative: Essential for resilience, competitive advantage, and attracting conscious consumers and investors in the modern market.
